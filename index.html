Game Title: The Haunting

The Haunting is a 3D horror/puzzle game in which you attempt to escape a haunted mansion by finding 5 crucifixes while evading a deadly priest that hunts for you. I wrote the code for the priest's AI, including it's hearing ability, its roaming, and its hunting and pathfinding ability. It relies on sound and sight, if it hears you or sees you in it's vision, it will hunt you! But fret not, you may still be able to hide!
